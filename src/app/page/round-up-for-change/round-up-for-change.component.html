What is Round for Change?

Round up for change is a voluntary program that "rounds up" your life purchases to the nearest dollar. We're exicted to partner with One Tree Planted and No Kid Hungry as We look to help the environment and child hunger.

How it works:

  • Each time you make a purchase with your Lifestyle Benefits card, your change will be rounded up to the charity you have chosen.

  • You'll be able to contribute as little as a penny or as much as 99 cents per life style purchase. If your purchase is $75.50, your purchase is rounded up to $76, with the extra 50 cents contributed to the charity you have chosen.



Where will your donations go to?


One Tree Planted

A non profit 501(c)3 environmental tree planting charity that plant trees in countries around the world.

No Kid Hungry

Working to end child hunger in the United States by ensuring that all children get the healthy food that they need to thrive.
